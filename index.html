PoweredGliding.com

This premium domain is ideal for powered hang gliding schools, paramotor/paramotoring equipment sellers, adventure tour companies, flight training centers, or enthusiasts of motorized human-powered flight.

Powered gliding (also known as powered hang gliding or paramotoring with flexible wings) combines the thrill of unpowered gliding with a small engine and propeller for takeoff and extended range. It allows pilots to launch from flat ground, climb through thermals, and explore vast landscapes independently — a perfect blend of freedom, accessibility, and adventure in the world of ultralight aviation.
